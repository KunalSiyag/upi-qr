# Pro UPI QR — Universal UPI QR Code Generator
> https://www.proupiqr.in/

## Overview
Pro UPI QR (proupiqr.in) is the leading free, privacy-first, client-side Universal UPI QR Code Generator and payment poster builder designed for Indian retail stores, kirana shops, freelancers, restaurants, NGOs, and individual bank account holders.

It generates standard-compliant `upi://pay` Unified Resource Identifiers (URIs) defined by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI). All QR code vectors are rendered 100% locally inside the user's browser using JavaScript. No Virtual Payment Addresses (VPAs), payee names, transaction notes, or amounts are ever sent to or stored on remote servers.

## Key Features & Capabilities
- **Universal Multi-App Support**: QR codes generated work across all Indian UPI applications including Google Pay (GPay), PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM UPI, CRED, Mobikwik, Navi, and banking apps (YONO SBI, HDFC Mobile, ICICI iMobile, Axis Bank, PNB).
- **Direct Bank Account Connection**: Allows linking any personal savings or current bank account VPA (e.g. `name@sbi`, `name@okicici`, `name@ybl`) to accept payments without merchant gateway fees (0% MDR).
- **Printable Poster Templates**: Offers 8+ counter-ready printable poster designs:
  - Shop Checkout Standee
  - Temple / NGO Donation Poster
  - Restaurant Table QR Tent Card
  - Freelancer Client Billing Card
  - Event Registration Pass
  - Tuition & Coaching Fee Collector
  - Cab / Taxi Dashboard Display Card
  - Custom Executive Minimal Card
- **Universal Non-Payment QR Generator**: Supports static QR creation for URLs, PDFs, vCard contact cards, WiFi auto-connect credentials, SMS, Email, and App Store links.
- **Invoice PDF Generator**: Embedded UPI QR code builder inside GST-compliant digital invoices for freelancers and billing counters.
