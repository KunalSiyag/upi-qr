Every major Indian bank offers a **free UPI QR** when you open a merchant or business account — or sometimes even via retail banking apps. Should you use that plain sticker or a custom standee from [Pro UPI QR](/)?

---

## How to Get Bank-Issued UPI QR

### SBI
* **YONO SBI / YONO Business** → Merchant section → Generate/Download UPI QR.
* Physical vinyl sticker via branch request for current account holders.

### HDFC
* **HDFC SmartHub Vyapar** or merchant portal → UPI QR download.
* Branch-assisted QR for SmartHub POS merchants.

### ICICI
* **InstaBIZ** app → Collections → UPI QR.
* Print or order standee through relationship manager for larger merchants.

### Axis Bank
* **Axis Mobile / Bharat Pe partnerships** (varies) — check Axis merchant dashboard.

### PhonePe / Paytm / GPay Business
Not banks, but free merchant QRs with business app KYC — see activation guides for [PhonePe](/blog/phonepe-business-qr-code-activation/), [GPay](/blog/google-pay-business-qr-code-activation/), [Paytm](/blog/paytm-business-qr-soundbox-charges/).

## Bank QR vs Custom Pro UPI QR Standee

| Factor | Free bank QR | Custom Pro UPI QR |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Cost | Free | Free generator + print ₹20–₹100 |
| Design | Plain QR + logo | Branded templates, standee layout |
| Same UPI interoperability | Yes | Yes |
| Template choice | Limited | Shop, salon, donation, taxi, etc. |
| Update when VPA changes | Re-download from bank | Regenerate in 30 seconds |
| Amount pre-fill | Usually static only | Static + [dynamic amount](/blog/how-to-generate-upi-qr-with-amount/) |

**Important:** Both encode standard `upi://pay` — customers can scan with any UPI app. See [Paytm vs PhonePe vs GPay](/blog/paytm-vs-phonepe-vs-gpay-which-qr-best-for-shop/).

## Best Practice: Use Both

1. **Register merchant VPA** with bank (correct legal name).
2. **Download bank QR** as backup reference.
3. **Generate matching custom standee** on Pro UPI QR with **same VPA**.
4. [Verify](/blog/how-to-verify-upi-qr-code-before-displaying/) both scan to identical payee name.
5. Display custom standee customer-facing; keep bank PDF in office records.

## When Bank QR Alone Is Enough

* Temporary market stall
* Staff-less donation box with minimal branding need
* You already ordered free PhonePe physical standee and can wait 7–10 days
