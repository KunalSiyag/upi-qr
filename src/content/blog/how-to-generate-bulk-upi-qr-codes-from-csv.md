### Step-by-Step Guide to Generate Bulk UPI QR Codes

Follow these steps using our free online batch generator:

1. **Prepare Your Excel / CSV File:** Create a spreadsheet with 4 standard columns:
   * **Column 1:** Payee Name (e.g. `Flat 101 Maintenance`)
   * **Column 2:** Merchant UPI ID (e.g. `society@oksbi`)
   * **Column 3:** Amount in ₹ (e.g. `2500`)
   * **Column 4:** Transaction Note (e.g. `July Fee #101`)
2. **Save as CSV:** In Excel or Google Sheets, click `File > Download > Comma Separated Values (.csv)`.
3. **Open the Bulk QR Generator:** Go to the [Free Bulk UPI QR Generator](/bulk-qr/).
4. **Upload or Paste CSV:** Click **Upload CSV File** or paste the raw CSV text directly into the input console.
5. **Batch Validation:** The tool validates all UPI IDs and highlights any formatting errors automatically.
6. **Download ZIP Archive:** Click **Download ZIP** to save high-resolution PNG QR images for every record in your spreadsheet.
