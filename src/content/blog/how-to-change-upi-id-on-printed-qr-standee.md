Changed your bank account? Got a new merchant VPA from PhonePe Business? Your **old printed QR still points to the previous UPI ID** until you replace it — customers will keep paying the old address.

---

## When You Must Replace the Standee

* Switched primary bank (e.g., SBI → HDFC).
* New merchant VPA from business app (`shop@ybl` → `shop@okbizaxis`).
* Closed personal UPI after registering business account.
* Suspected QR tampering or fraud.

You cannot "edit" a printed QR — you **regenerate and reprint**.

---

## Step-by-Step Replacement

1. **Deactivate old VPA** in banking app if possible, or remove old standee immediately.
2. Confirm **new UPI ID** and **payee name** in bank app.
3. Open [Pro UPI QR Generator](/) with new details.
4. Download fresh PNG (same template as before for brand consistency).
5. [Verify with ₹1 test](/blog/how-to-verify-upi-qr-code-before-displaying/) from two different UPI apps.
6. **Destroy** old cards — shred or black out QR module so staff does not mix them up.
7. Deploy new standee; inform regular customers via WhatsApp if needed.

---

## Multi-Counter Shops

If you have 3 billing counters with old QR:
* Reprint all on same day — mixed old/new causes accounting chaos.
* Consider [separate QR per counter](/blog/how-to-generate-upi-qr-for-multiple-staff-counters/) with notes `Counter1`, `Counter2`.
