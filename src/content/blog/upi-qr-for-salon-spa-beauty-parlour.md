Salon bills combine services, products, and tips — slowing checkout when only cash is accepted. A **UPI QR at the billing mirror** speeds payment while customers check their haircut.

---

## Recommended Setup

1. **Reception standee** — static QR for variable final bill (customer enters amount shown on bill).
2. **Package board** — dynamic QRs for fixed combos (`Hair Spa ₹1,499`, `Bridal Trial ₹3,500`).
3. **Stylist tip jar alternative** — small QR card labelled "Tips — Thank You" with static QR to salon VPA (optional separate tips VPA).

Generate branded cards on [Pro UPI QR](/) using Shop or Freelancer templates.

---

## Billing Flow

1. Staff writes bill total on paper or POS.
2. Customer scans standee QR OR package-specific dynamic QR.
3. Customer verifies **salon name** on UPI screen before PIN.
4. Front desk confirms credit notification before customer leaves.

[Verify payee name guide](/blog/how-to-verify-upi-qr-code-before-displaying/).

## Multi-Stylist Reconciliation

| Model | How |
| :--- | :--- |
| Single salon VPA | Simple; split commissions internally |
| Per-stylist VPAs | Each stylist QR at station — clearer splits |
| Package dynamic QR | Includes service code in `tn` field |

For per-stylist QR at 5 chairs, use [bulk QR workflow](/blog/how-to-generate-bulk-upi-qr-codes/).

## GST and Invoices

* UPI QR collects payment — still issue GST invoice if registered.
* Mention GST-inclusive total on bill before customer pays.
* Store UTR on invoice copy for audits.
