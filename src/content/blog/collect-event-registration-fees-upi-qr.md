Organizing a college fest, a local charity run, a workshop, or a community meetup requires a smooth registry and ticketing system. Standard online ticketing portals charge up to 5% to 10% ticketing commissions.

By combining Google Forms with a custom UPI QR code, you can build a 100% free, commission-free event registry. Here is how.

---

## Why Avoid Standard Ticketing Gateways?

For small meetups or charity events, ticketing portals eat into your margins. If you sell 200 tickets at ₹500 each, a gateway fee of 5% means you lose ₹5,000. 
Using a direct UPI QR code ensures **every single rupee** is deposited directly into your team's bank account instantly.

---

## Step-by-Step Setup Guide

### 1. Build a Google Form for Registration
Create a standard registration form asking for:
* Full Name and Email Address
* Phone Number / WhatsApp
* Ticket Category / Preferences

### 2. Generate a Dynamic QR Code
Go to **Pro UPI QR**, enter your bank VPA, and set the ticket amount (e.g., ₹500) and note (e.g., "TechFest Entry"). Download the high-resolution QR poster.

### 3. Embed the QR Code inside the Google Form
Add an **Image Section** right before the submit button in your Google Form showing the QR card. Add instructions:
> *"Scan the QR code above and transfer ₹500. Copy the 12-digit UPI Transaction ID (UTR) and upload the payment confirmation screenshot below to verify your ticket."*

### 4. Add Verification Fields
Add two required fields to your form:
* **UPI Transaction ID (UTR):** A 12-digit number (e.g. `3129...`).
* **Screenshot Upload:** A file upload question to check the payment status visually.

---

## Tips for Event Days

On the day of the event, print out several **A4 Size Pro UPI QR posters** and place them at the entry gates. This allows walk-in guests to scan, pay, and show the screen to your volunteers for instant entry, preventing long queues at the registration counter.
