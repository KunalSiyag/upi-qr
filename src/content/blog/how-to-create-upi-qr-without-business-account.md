You do not need a Paytm Business, PhonePe Business, or corporate current account to generate a scannable UPI QR code. If you have any active UPI ID linked to your bank account — even a personal one — you can build a payment QR in minutes.

This guide covers when that is fine, when you should upgrade, and how to print a professional standee anyway.

---

## What You Actually Need

1. A bank account with UPI enabled (SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, etc.).
2. A UPI ID (VPA) such as `yourname@okicici`, `mobile@ybl`, or `shop@oksbi`.
3. A tool to encode the VPA into a QR image — like the [Pro UPI QR Generator](/).

No GST registration, no business KYC app, and no payment gateway signup is required for a basic QR poster.

---

## Step-by-Step: Personal UPI QR for a Small Shop

1. Open your banking or UPI app and confirm your **primary UPI ID** and **registered payee name** (the name customers see before paying).
2. Go to [Pro UPI QR Generator](/).
3. Enter the **Payee Name** exactly as shown in your bank app.
4. Enter your **UPI ID / VPA**.
5. Pick a template (Shop Standee, Freelancer Card, etc.) and download the PNG.

Test by scanning with [Google Pay](/google-pay-qr-generator/) or [PhonePe](/phonepe-qr-generator/) before placing it on your counter.

## Limits and Risks of Personal UPI IDs

| Topic | Personal UPI | Merchant / Business UPA |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Daily receive limit | Often ₹1–2 lakh/day (bank-dependent) | Higher, configurable with bank |
| Tax / compliance | Fine for micro sales; track income for ITR | Better for registered businesses |
| Customer trust | Name may show personal name | Shows business/trust name |
| Chargebacks / disputes | Limited merchant tooling | Business apps offer settlement reports |

For a home bakery, tutor, or weekend stall, personal UPI is common. For a high-volume retail store or registered company, open a **current account** and request a **merchant VPA** from your bank. Read [understanding UPI VPA and merchant accounts](/blog/understanding-upi-vpa-merchant-accounts/).

---

## When to Upgrade to a Business Account

Upgrade if you:
* Receive more than ₹1 lakh/day regularly.
* Need staff-wise settlement reports or soundbox alerts.
* Operate as a registered company, trust, or GST-registered firm.
* Want Google Maps / PhonePe discovery features.

You can still use **Pro UPI QR** with your new merchant VPA — just update the UPI ID on a fresh print. See [how to change UPI ID on a printed standee](/blog/how-to-change-upi-id-on-printed-qr-standee/).
