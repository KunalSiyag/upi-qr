When a customer says "QR not working," the problem is usually physical print quality or lighting — not UPI itself. Work through this list from fastest fix to full reprint.

---

## Quick Fixes (Try First)

1. **Wipe the standee** — grease and fingerprints block cameras.
2. **Tilt away from ceiling LED** — glare from glossy laminate is the #1 scan killer. Switch to matte.
3. **Increase phone brightness** — ask the customer to raise screen brightness (helps their camera, not yours, but often works).
4. **Try a second app** — test with [Google Pay](/google-pay-qr-generator/), [PhonePe](/phonepe-qr-generator/), and [Paytm](/paytm-qr-generator/).
5. **Move standee closer** — if QR is under 2 cm, customers cannot scan from across the counter.

## Print and Size Problems

| Symptom | Likely cause | Fix |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Works on screen, not on paper | Low DPI print | Reprint at 300 DPI from [Pro UPI QR](/) PNG |
| Works close, fails at distance | QR too small | See [size dimensions guide](/blog/upi-qr-code-size-dimensions-printing-guide/) |
| Partial scan spin, no open | Damaged/creased QR | Replace card |
| Works in morning, not under noon sun | Glare on gloss laminate | Matte laminate or acrylic tent |

---

## Data and App Problems

* **Invalid VPA:** QR encodes wrong UPI ID — regenerate from bank-confirmed VPA.
* **Amount too long:** Very large `am` values or special characters in `tn` can break some older apps — simplify the note field.
* **Pending payments:** If payment hangs after scan, see [resolve UPI pending transactions](/blog/how-to-resolve-upi-pending-transaction-issues/).
* **Customer on old app version:** Ask them to update GPay/PhonePe.

---

## Counter Placement Tips

* Place QR at **chest height**, facing the customer.
* Avoid glass reflections directly behind the QR.
* For outdoor stalls, use [waterproof sticker guidance](/blog/how-to-print-durable-waterproof-qr-stickers/).
* At night markets, add a small LED on the standee — not on the QR surface.

---

## When to Reprint Entirely

Reprint if:
* You changed bank account or UPI ID.
* Fraudster sticker was found on your standee.
* QR module is scratched through lamination.
