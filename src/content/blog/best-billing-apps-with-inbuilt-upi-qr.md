For retail supermarkets, apparel stores, and pharmacies, printing a separate QR standee card is only half the solution. In high-traffic environments, you need to print a unique QR code on the invoice receipt itself, showing the exact billing amount for that transaction.

In this guide, we review the best Indian retail billing apps that support native UPI QR printing on thermal receipts.

---

## 1. Vyapar (Excellent for Small Businesses)
Vyapar is a highly popular GST billing and inventory app. It has built-in support for UPI payments:
* **How it works:** You can link your VPA in the settings. When you create an invoice, Vyapar automatically generates a dynamic QR containing the invoice amount and prints it at the bottom of the PDF or thermal receipt.
* **Platforms:** Available for Windows and Android.

---

## 2. MyBillBook (Cloud-Based Billing)
MyBillBook is an easy-to-use cloud accounting software designed for distributors and retailers:
* **Features:** Supports generating both static QR codes for shop counters and dynamic payment links that can be sent directly to customers via SMS or WhatsApp.
* **Reconciliation:** Instantly registers when a payment is completed.

---

## 3. TallyPrime (Best for Large Enterprises)
Tally is the gold standard for accounting in India. TallyPrime supports integrations with leading payment gateways to print dynamic UPI QR codes on standard tax invoices:
* **Benefits:** Absolute compliance with GST and e-invoicing mandates. Ideal for B2B distributors who receive large value payments.
