Shop owners often ask which app QR to display — Paytm, PhonePe, or Google Pay. The answer surprises most: **you usually need only one standard UPI QR**, not three separate stickers.

---

## The Key Fact: UPI Is Interoperable

NPCI's UPI standard means a QR encoding `upi://pay?pa=shop@bank&pn=Shop` opens in **any** compliant app — GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, bank apps.

You do **not** need three different QRs unless you want app-specific branding or separate settlement VPAs (rare for small shops).

---

## App-Specific Business QRs (When They Matter)

| App | Business product | Extra benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| PhonePe | PhonePe Business | Smartbox sound, settlement dashboard |
| Google Pay | GPay for Business | Maps visibility, merchant offers |
| Paytm | Paytm for Business | Soundbox, Paytm Mall listing |

These give **merchant tools**, not a different payment rail. Money still settles to your linked bank via UPI.

## When to Display Multiple QRs

* Different VPAs for different legal entities (rare).
* Franchise mandates specific app reporting.
* Marketing co-branding with Paytm/PhonePe signage programs.

Otherwise one QR reduces counter clutter and [tampering risk](/blog/prevent-upi-qr-code-tampering-frauds/).
