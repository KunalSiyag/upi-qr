## Executive Summary: Google Pay vs PhonePe vs Paytm

If you are looking for a quick decision:
* **Interoperability:** Every standard UPI QR code is fully interoperable. A single QR can accept payments from GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, and 80+ banking apps.
* **Transaction Fees:** UPI transactions from a bank account or pre-paid wallet (up to ₹2,000) have **0% MDR (Zero fees)**.
* **Best for Storefront Visibility:** Google Pay is excellent for local SEO, placing your shop on Google Maps.
* **Best for Audio Alerts:** PhonePe and Paytm are neck-and-neck with soundbox offerings, though PhonePe features lower rental schemes in many regions.

## Detailed Feature Deep-Dive

### 1. Google Pay for Business (GPay)
Google Pay is a strong option for merchant setups because of its integration with the Google ecosystem.
* **Local SEO Benefit:** Setting up a GPay Business profile automatically lists your shop on Google Maps, helping you gain organic search footfall from users searching for "shops near me".
* **Settlement:** Google Pay settles payments instantly. Funds go directly to your linked bank account via peer-to-peer (P2P) or peer-to-merchant (P2M) transfer rails.
* **Soundbox Option:** GPay offers "Google Pay Soundpod" in selected cities with dynamic voice notifications.

*Guide:* [How to activate Google Pay Business QR](/blog/google-pay-business-qr-code-activation/)

### 2. PhonePe Business
PhonePe is currently India's market leader in UPI transaction volume.
* **High Customer Trust:** Because a massive percentage of UPI users run PhonePe, having a PhonePe branded sticker gives consumers familiarity.
* **PhonePe Smartbox:** PhonePe's audio box is highly reliable and is offered with flexible rental plans (often starting at ₹49/month or waived during promotional periods).
* **Instant Settlements:** PhonePe allows manual settlement trigger ("Settle Now") in the merchant app for real-time clearance, otherwise settles overnight by default.

*Guide:* [How to activate PhonePe Business QR](/blog/phonepe-business-qr-code-activation/)

### 3. Paytm for Business
Paytm is the pioneer of QR payments and audio soundboxes in India.
* **Soundbox Ecosystem:** Paytm offers the most advanced soundboxes, including versions with calculator keypads, LCD screens showing transaction amounts, and multi-language support.
* **Paytm Business Dashboard:** Offers comprehensive bookkeeping, loan eligibility tracking, and vendor payout solutions.
* **Settlement Modes:** Supports instant settlement, twice-a-day settlement, or next-morning settlement.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1. Is there any commission or charge on UPI QR code payments?
No. For standard UPI transactions funded by bank accounts or UPI wallets, there is 0% Merchant Discount Rate (MDR). Both you and the customer pay ₹0 fees. For Rupay credit card payments via UPI, MDR is 0% for transactions up to ₹2,000.

### Q2. Can a customer scan a PhonePe QR using the GPay app?
Yes, absolutely. Since all these apps comply with the NPCI interoperability framework, a PhonePe merchant QR can be scanned by Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM, or any bank UPI app.

### Q3. How do I prevent QR code standee tampering and fraud?
Displaying a single, custom-designed QR with your shop's official name makes it easy for you and your customers to verify the payee name during checkout. Check out our guide on [preventing QR tampering and billing scams](/blog/prevent-upi-qr-code-tampering-frauds/).
