PhonePe is one of the most widely used consumer payment apps in India. For shopkeepers and service providers, having a PhonePe Business account is a great way to accept payments and manage daily payouts.

This guide outlines how to register for a PhonePe Business merchant account and acquire your official QR code standee.

---

## Benefits of PhonePe Business for Merchants

* **Zero Transaction Cost:** Direct-to-bank UPI transfers remain completely free for merchants.
* **Dedicated Business App:** Monitor your daily settlements, view customer payment histories, and manage staff access.
* **Voice Alerts:** Option to set up the PhonePe Smartbox for loud payment verification announcements.

---

## Steps to Register and Activate Your Account

1. Download the **PhonePe Business** app from the app store.
2. Enter your business mobile number and verify it using the OTP sent to your phone.
3. Enter your business name, category, and address.
4. Provide your Bank Account details where you want the payouts to be settled. You will need your Account Number and IFSC Code.
5. Complete the basic KYC by providing your PAN card number. Once verified, your account is activated instantly, and a virtual QR code is created.

---

## Getting a Physical PhonePe Standee

Once your digital profile is active, you can order a physical QR standee card from inside the PhonePe Business app. Tap on **Manage QR Codes**, select **Request New Standee**, and enter your shipping address. PhonePe will ship a physical standee to your store, usually within 7-10 working days. Alternatively, you can copy your merchant VPA and paste it into the **Pro UPI QR Generator** to create a custom-designed printable standee instantly.
