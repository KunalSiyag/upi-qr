Google Pay (GPay) is a dominant player in the Indian UPI ecosystem. Setting up a Google Pay for Business account allows you to leverage their secure infrastructure to receive money directly into your bank account.

Here is the step-by-step walkthrough to get your business verified on Google Pay.

---

## Features of Google Pay for Business

* **Direct Integration:** Seamless connection with Google Maps, helping nearby customers discover your shop.
* **Detailed Analytics:** Track weekly and monthly sales trends via clean graphs.
* **Rewards & Scratch Cards:** Access to Google merchant cashback campaigns and merchant rewards.

---

## Activation Guide

1. Download the **Google Pay for Business** app from the Play Store or App Store.
2. Sign in using your Google Account.
3. Tap on **Set up as a business** and enter your registered business name, sole proprietorship details, and category.
4. Input your shop address and pinpoint its location on the map.
5. Provide your bank account details and upload a photo of your PAN card to complete the merchant verification process.
6. GPay will perform a small validation deposit (usually ₹1) to verify your account. Once verified, your QR code will be ready.

---

## Fetching Your GPay QR Code

You can view your QR code under the **QR Code** tab on the app dashboard. You can download the image directly to your phone.
