Cash-on-delivery creates change problems, fake note risk, and rider safety issues. A **laminated UPI QR card** on the delivery bag or bike lets customers pay the exact order balance in seconds.

---

## Setup for Individual Riders

1. Use your UPI ID (prefer dedicated rider/settlement VPA if fleet-managed).
2. Generate **Cab / Taxi Dashboard Card** or compact minimal template on [Pro UPI QR](/).
3. Laminate A7/A6 card, zip-tie to delivery box interior.
4. For order-specific amounts, show **dynamic QR on phone screen** from generator with `am=orderTotal`.

Fleet operators: assign per-rider VPA for reconciliation — [multi-counter guide](/blog/how-to-generate-upi-qr-for-multiple-staff-counters/).

---

## Workflow per Delivery

1. Customer orders via phone — confirm total including delivery fee.
2. At doorstep: show QR (printed static for round amounts, or dynamic on phone for exact bill).
3. Customer scans, pays, shows success screen.
4. Rider marks delivered — no cash change needed.

For WhatsApp-order kitchens, send QR in chat before dispatch — [WhatsApp payment guide](/blog/how-to-create-whatsapp-payment-link-upi/).

---

## COD vs UPI QR

| Factor | Cash COD | UPI QR |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Change handling | Required | None |
| Fake currency risk | Yes | No |
| Rider theft/loss risk | Higher cash | Digital trail |
| Customer proof | Cash memo | UPI UTR screenshot |
| Works offline | Yes | Needs mobile data |

## Tips

* [Waterproof laminate](/blog/how-to-print-durable-waterproof-qr-stickers/) for monsoon.
* Bright sun glare — matte card, shade phone screen when showing dynamic QR.
* Never ask customer to scan QR to **receive** refund — scam pattern ([security guide](/blog/is-it-safe-to-scan-upi-qr-code/)).
* [Pending payment troubleshooting](/blog/how-to-resolve-upi-pending-transaction-issues/) if network fails — keep UPI ID text backup.
