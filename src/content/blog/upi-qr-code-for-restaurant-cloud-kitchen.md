Restaurants juggle dine-in bills, Swiggy/Zomato settlements, and direct WhatsApp orders. A **direct UPI QR** on every table and delivery bag captures walk-in and own-channel orders without aggregator commission.

---

## Three QR Placements

### 1. Table Tent (Dine-In)
* **Restaurant Table QR** template on [Pro UPI QR](/).
* Acrylic A6 tent with table number printed (`Table 7`).
* Static QR — customer pays bill amount entered by waiter or self-entered.

### 2. Fixed-Price Menu Items
* Chai ₹30, lunch thali ₹120 — use [dynamic amount QR](/blog/how-to-generate-upi-qr-with-amount/) per item on menu board.
* Reduces underpayment errors at self-service counters.

### 3. Cloud Kitchen / WhatsApp Orders
* Send dynamic QR in order confirmation WhatsApp with exact order total + `tn=Order882`.
* Stick mini QR on delivery bag for cashless tip or balance payment.

---

## UPI vs Aggregator Settlement

| Channel | Commission | Settlement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Swiggy/Zomato | 18–28%+ | Weekly, deductions |
| Direct UPI QR | Free (bank UPI) | Instant bank credit |

Many restaurants push repeat customers to WhatsApp ordering with UPI QR to save aggregator fees.

---

## Operations

* Separate VPAs for **dine-in** vs **catering** if books differ.
* Train staff: confirm UPI credit before closing table — soundbox optional ([free soundbox setup](/blog/setup-soundbox-announcements-for-shop-free/)).
* [Waterproof stickers](/blog/how-to-print-durable-waterproof-qr-stickers/) for takeaway bags.
* Social promotion: [Instagram/WhatsApp QR](/blog/how-to-add-upi-qr-to-instagram-whatsapp-status/).

---

## Hygiene and Placement

* Laminated table tents — wipeable.
* QR away from grease splash zone on kitchen pass — print spare tents monthly.
